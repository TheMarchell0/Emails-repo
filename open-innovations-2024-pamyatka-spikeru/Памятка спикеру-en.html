DEAR PARTICIPANT!
The Open Innovations Forum starts this Wednesday, and we cannot wait to meet you in Technopark "Skolkovo": Bolshoy Boulevard, 42, page 1, Skolkovo Technopark.
WHEN AND WHERE DO I PARTICIPATE?
If you have no information about your participation in business program, please, visit the Speakers section on the website: you can find all sessions where you participate, as well as time and location. All this information is also available in your account in My timetable.
We kindly recommend you to get to the forum at least an hour before the opening of your session. You will be able to prepare for the performance in the speaker's room. Remember to get closer to the stage in the hall where your session is held 15 minutes before the start.
